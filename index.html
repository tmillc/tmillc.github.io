ersilia.city - A simulated city

Utilizing historical data, procedural generation, statistical methods and machine learning techniques, Ersilia (named in homage to Italo Calvino's book, Invisible Cities) will be an on-going project with increasing scope, meant to provide data, images, reports, events, etc. on the population, weather, geography, and development of a simulated city.
